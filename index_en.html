About Me

After years of being unable to practice sports due to an injury, I faced my biggest challenge: knee surgery. Instead of giving up, I worked hard and with discipline, and a year later, I was back in competition, achieving three first-place finishes in a row.

Today, my focus has evolved. Although competitions are no longer my priority, my goal is still to improve my performance, strengthen my body, and optimize my health. I am building a solid foundation to, in the future, return to the highest level.

My experience has taught me that with consistency, hard work, and the right strategy, any obstacle can be overcome. That's what I want to convey to everyone who trains with me at WaveFit, helping them reach their best version.
